About

Hello, my name is Petr, and I'm a 21-year-old computer science student. I have a passion for technology and I love exploring and learning new things in the field.

One of my biggest hobbies is homelabbing, which involves building and configuring my own servers and networks at home. I enjoy working with tools such as Proxmox, Docker, and TrueNAS to create unique and powerful computing environments. It's a great way to test and learn new skills while building something useful for myself and others.

Aside from technology, I also enjoy playing video games and reading about science fiction and fantasy. I'm always looking for new ways to challenge myself and learn, whether it's through a new programming language or a new strategy game.

While I don't consider myself a social butterfly, I am always open to meeting new people who share my interests. If you're also into homelabbing or computer science, don't hesitate to drop me a message and we can chat!
